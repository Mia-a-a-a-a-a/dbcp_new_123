DBCP: Supporting Research, Advancing Ocean Observations

DBCP Related Publications

Please note that this is not an exhaustive list. It includes only the publications reported by DBCP members who responded to our request for information regarding any publications they have authored or are aware of that are related to DBCP activities. The DBCP acknowledge that there are many more publications beyond those listed here.

If your publication is missing, please contact the DBCP secretariat support (WMO) with all corresponding references: Author(s), year, title, journal, and URL to an online library if available.
